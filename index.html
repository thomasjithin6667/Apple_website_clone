Meet the
redesigned iPad.

The all-new iPad is colorfully reimagined to be more capable, more intuitive, and even more fun. With a new all‑screen design, 10.9-inch Liquid Retina display, and four gorgeous colors, iPad delivers a powerful way to get things done, create, and stay connected.1 Add on essential accessories designed just for iPad and enjoy endless versatility for everything you love to do.
Draw, paint, and write with Apple Pencil. Type comfortably, use a trackpad, and enjoy content with the versatile two-piece design of the Magic Keyboard Folio.2 You can use familiar keyboard shortcuts or the click-anywhere trackpad. And get an incredible typing experience.

Amazing apps? Apps‑olutely.

iPad comes with powerful and capable apps like Photos, Maps, Messages, Apple News, Mail, and Safari. And with over a million apps on the App Store designed just for iPad, you’ll find the right app for anything you want to do. Manage a project with Trello, collaborate on the infinite canvas of Freeform, or finish your term paper with Microsoft Word.5
